About Shree Vaastu

Our journey in bringing harmony to spaces

Our Philosophy

At Shree Vaastu, we believe that your living and working spaces should support and enhance your life, not work against it. Vaastu Shastra, the ancient Indian science of architecture and space, provides timeless principles for creating harmonious environments.

Founded in 2010 by renowned Vaastu expert Rajesh Sharma, Shree Vaastu has helped over 1,500 clients transform their homes and businesses through proper space alignment.

Our Approach

We combine traditional Vaastu principles with modern architectural understanding to provide practical solutions that work in today's world. Our consultations are customized to each client's unique situation, needs, and constraints.

Our Mission

To help individuals and businesses create spaces that promote health, happiness, prosperity and success by aligning with natural energies and cosmic principles.

Rajesh Sharma

Founder & Chief Vaastu Consultant

With over 20 years of experience in Vaastu Shastra, Rajesh Sharma has dedicated his life to studying and applying this ancient science. He holds a Ph.D. in Vedic Sciences and has trained under several renowned Vaastu masters in India.
